What we do

Web and Mobile/

Website development, Ecommerce platforms, CRM, Content Management Systems, Android apps, iOS apps. We partner with you to build the software products you need to succeed in your business. You could be in the ideation phase looking to bring your product to life or at hyper-growth stage expecting to enhance your product capabilities. Our expertise across the development life cycle will add value to your product. We help you create UI/UX Definition, Software Definition, develop your Software product, launch/deploy your product as well as maintain it. Our Agile development methodology ensure that you have firm control on what is being built. The consultative approach that we take will enable you to go-to-market quicker.

Product Prototyping/

You are an early stage business and want to test out your concept quickly. Work with us to create a lightweight implementation of your idea that helps you evaluate and affirm it. The prototypes allow you to visualize the application, make it easier to make design decisions without investing in building the system. Early views of future applications lead to fewer changes later and hence reduce overall costs considerably.

Cloud and DevOps/

Migration to Cloud, AWS cloud management (instance and application) using Chef, puppet, Deployment & CI using Jenkins, Hudson. DevOps is a new discipline that complements the Agile Delivery models for software engineering by bringing in Automation and Monitoring on the IT infrastructure by leveraging Cloud computing concepts. DevOps leverages collaboration, tool-chain pipelines, automation and Cloud adoption. The immediate benefits can be observed as improved processes, more standardizations and building trust and improve productivity across development, QA and IT operations teams. We create deployment strategies, deploy your application and also monitor it. We enable you to have maximum uptime and availability.
